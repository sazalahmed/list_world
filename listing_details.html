Kecamatan Mengwi, Cozy 3 BR Pool Villa Canggu Bali Indonesia.

Review (237)

Overview

This stunning beachfront villa is the perfect place to relax and unwind in paradise. With its own private swimming pool, jacuzzi, and gym, you'll have everything you need for a truly unforgettable vacation.

The villa is located on a secluded stretch of beach, with breathtaking views of the Caribbean Sea. The interior is spacious and airy, with modern furnishings and all the amenities you could need.

On the ground floor, you'll find a large living room and dining area, opening out onto the pool terrace. There's also a fully equipped kitchen, a laundry room, and a guest bedroom.Upstairs, you'll find the master suite, with its own private balcony and bathroom. There are also two other bedrooms, each with their own en-suite bathroom.

The pool terrace is the perfect place to relax and soak up the sun. There's a large swimming pool, a jacuzzi, and plenty of sun loungers. There's also a barbecue area, where you can cook delicious meals to enjoy al fresco.
